# Code of Conduct

Desktop Pet aims to be a welcoming project for people learning, experimenting,
and contributing at different skill levels.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, and ask clarifying questions when something is unclear.
- Keep feedback specific to the code, documentation, design, or behavior.
- Make room for new contributors.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Dismissive or insulting comments about a person's background or experience.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing project discussions.

## Reporting

If you maintain a public fork, add a project-specific contact method here before
publishing. Maintainers should review reports promptly, protect reporters from
retaliation, and take proportionate action when needed.
